# Business Goals, Drivers and Requirements

## Background

StayHealthy, Inc. is a large and highly successful medical software company located in San Francisco, California, US. They are now expanding into the medical monitoring market, and is in need of a new medical patient monitoring system for hospitals that monitors a patients vital signs using proprietary medical monitoring devices built by StayHealthy, Inc.

### StayHealthy, Inc.
StayHealthy. Inc. will be providing a comprehensive hardware and software for this on premise monitoring system 


## Business Goals
StayHealthy, Inc. is now expanding into the medical monitoring market, and is in need of a new medical patient monitoring system for hospitals that monitors a patients vital signs using proprietary medical monitoring devices built by StayHealthy, Inc.
Thus, the overall goal of StayHealthy is to gather, store and analyze patient's health records end-to-end

## Business Drivers 

1. StayHealthy, Inc. has to expand into the medical monitoring market
2. Provide a new medical patient monitoring system for hospitals.
3. Monitors patients vital signs 
4. Allow medical professional to review vital history, filtering and upload the data to existing cloud based medical record system
5. Analyze each patient’s vital signs and alert a medical professional if it detects an issue


# Requirements and Engagement model

## Engagement Models

### Sensors

Send patient's vital data to StayHealthy's monitoring tool


### MonitorMe 

- Read sensor data
- Send consolidated data to nurse station
- Analyse patient vitals
- Alert medical professional for issues   

### Nurse station

- Display patient vitals rotated every 5 seconds
- Receive alerts notification

### Medical professional

- Review vital reading for patient
- Take snapshots of reading , filter by vital type, time range
- Upload patient snapshot to MyMedicalData
